Freelance Field Engineer (Event WiFi)

On-site • Freelance / contract • Weekends sometimes
Apply via email

You deploy and operate the on-site network stack: mounting APs, setting up routing/uplinks, verifying coverage, and keeping the event live.

Responsibilities
  • Build AP layouts, mount hardware, run safe cabling, label and document.
  • Bring up WAN links (Starlink, 5G/LTE, fiber/cable when present) and verify failover.
  • Validate RF and client experience; tune channels, power, and QoS profiles.
  • Coordinate with organizers and vendors (POS, ticketing, livestream teams).
Requirements
  • Hands-on networking basics: VLANs, DHCP/DNS, routing, troubleshooting.
  • Comfortable working outdoors and lifting/handling equipment safely.
  • Clear, calm communication under time pressure.
  • Driver’s license and ability to travel in NL/DE/BE.

NOC Support (On-call)

Remote • Freelance / contract • Evenings & weekends
Apply via email

You monitor live deployments, respond to alerts, and help field engineers resolve incidents quickly (connectivity, routing, RF, vendor issues).

Responsibilities
  • Watch dashboards, interpret telemetry, and triage alerts.
  • Guide on-site staff via chat/phone; keep incident notes and timelines.
  • Coordinate failovers and controlled changes (uplink swaps, QoS changes).
  • Post-event handoff: short incident summaries and improvements.
Requirements
  • Solid troubleshooting: TCP/IP, routing, DNS, WiFi basics.
  • Comfortable making safe changes under a change window.
  • Strong written communication (clear status updates).
  • Availability during some evenings/weekends (rotating schedule).

Sales / Account (Event Connectivity)

Remote + on-site visits • Freelance / contract • Commission-based
Apply via email

You help organizers and venues understand what they need, translate requirements into clear proposals, and keep communication sharp before and during an event.

Responsibilities
  • Qualify leads and collect requirements (date, venue, attendance, vendors).
  • Coordinate pricing and scope with ops; write proposals and SLAs.
  • Keep clients informed with simple, reliable timelines and expectations.

Requirements

  • Able to explain technical topics clearly without jargon.
  • Comfortable with customer calls and on-site visits.
  • German and/or Dutch is a plus; English required.
